Join the group

We are always interested in hearing from students and researchers who want to join us. We welcome inquiries from students interested in undergraduate theses, PhD projects and postdoctoral stays in soft matter theory and simulation. No previous simulation experience is required — we look for genuine interest in physical chemistry and willingness to learn computational tools.

If you are interested, feel free to reach out by email at any time.
