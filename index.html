Molecular Modeling Group

Modelado Molecular de Materiales Blandos y Nanoestructurados

We are a theory and computational simulation group at the Instituto de Investigaciones Fisicoquímicas Teóricas y Aplicadas (INIFTA, UNLP–CONICET). We develop and apply molecular models to understand how nanoscale interactions determine the macroscopic behavior of polymers, nanoparticles and interfaces.

What we do

Theory

Molecular models

We develop molecular mean-field theories and coarse-grained models to describe soft matter systems with molecular detail at an accessible computational cost.

Simulation

High-performance computing

We apply molecular dynamics and Monte Carlo simulations to explore the thermodynamics and structure of nanostructured materials.

Applications

From model to material

We work alongside experimental groups on the design of stimuli-responsive materials with biomedical and environmental applications.
