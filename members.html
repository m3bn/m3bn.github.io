Members

Principal investigator

Gabriel S. Longo

CONICET Researcher · Group leader

Gabriel’s research leverages molecular theory and modeling to explore the fascinating world of soft materials, specifically investigating how stimuli-responsive gels adapt and react to their environments.

PhD fellows and students

Néstor Ariel Pérez Chávez

CONICET Post Doc

Ariel’s current postdoctoral research focuses on the computational design of MOFs for VOC adsorption and capture. He uses molecular simulation and data analysis to link pore structure, host-guest interactions, and adsorption performance.

✉ Mail Scholar ORCID GitHub

Marilina Cathcarth

CONICET PhD student

Marilina uses molecular theory and coarse-grained models to understand how surface properties and solution conditions govern protein adsorption on silica nanoparticles, aiming to rationally design coatings with better antifouling and colloidal-stability properties.

✉ Mail Scholar ORCID GitHub

Ana María Cruz González

CONICET PhD student
